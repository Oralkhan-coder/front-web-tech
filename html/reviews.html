2025 Porsche 911 Turbo S: Perfection Refined

★★★★★ 9.2/10
By Marcus Johnson, Senior Editor
March 15, 2025

The 2025 Porsche 911 Turbo S continues to redefine what a sports car can be. With its twin-turbocharged flat-six engine producing 640 horsepower, this machine launches from 0-60 mph in just 2.6 seconds while maintaining the everyday usability that makes the 911 legendary. The PDK transmission shifts with telepathic precision, and the all-wheel-drive system provides confidence in any weather condition. Inside, the cabin blends classic 911 design elements with cutting-edge technology, including the new 12.3-inch infotainment display and premium materials throughout. This isn't just a car; it's automotive poetry in motion.
